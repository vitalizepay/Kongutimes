Salem

சேலம்
Steel City of Tamil Nadu · தமிழ்நாட்டின் எஃகு நகரம்

Home to the Salem Steel Plant and Mettur Dam — one of Tamil Nadu's most important water reservoirs. With 27.5 lakh voters across 10 seats, Salem is the largest district by voter count in the entire Kongu zone.

சேலம் எஃகு ஆலை மற்றும் மேட்டூர் அணை. 27.5 லட்சம் வாக்காளர்கள் கொண்ட இந்த மாவட்டம் கொங்கு மண்டலத்தில் மிகவும் முக்கியமானது.

Industry

Salem Steel Plant Phase 4 — 2,000 new jobs, capacity up 30%

The Phase 4 expansion of the Salem Steel Plant has been officially launched, increasing production capacity by 30%. The expansion is expected to create 2,000 direct jobs and around 8,000 indirect employment opportunities. The plant now ranks among India's top steel producers. The expansion was completed ahead of the 2026 election and is a major talking point in Salem's political campaigning.

Election 2026

Salem 10 constituencies — highest voter count in Kongu at 27.5 lakh

Salem district has the largest voter base in the Kongu zone with 27,55,830 registered voters across 10 assembly constituencies. Key contests include Salem West (PMK/NDA), Salem East (DMK), Edappadi and Omalur. Congress has been allocated the Erode East seat in alliance sharing, while TVK contests all seats as an independent force.
