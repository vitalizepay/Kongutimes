Election 2026 • Exclusive

Erode East battle heats up: DMK fields youth leader, AIADMK bets on ex-mayor

With just 10 days to go for polling, Erode East has turned into a prestige fight. DMK has fielded 32-year-old entrepreneur K. Senthil Kumar, while AIADMK’s candidate is former corporation mayor R. Manimaran. NTK’s A. Kavya is emerging as a strong third factor, especially among first-time voters. The constituency saw a heated roadshow on Sunday with thousands of cadres from both parties. Independent analysts suggest a close margin of under 8,000 votes.

Infrastructure • Daily Brief

Perundurai textile park gets ₹450 crore push – 12,000 jobs expected

The state government has given final clearance to the Perundurai Integrated Textile Park, a project that will span 280 acres along the Erode–Chennai highway. With an investment of ₹450 crore from the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation and private textile groups, the park is expected to generate 12,000 direct jobs. Construction will begin in June 2026, with the first phase operational by March 2028. Local power loom weavers have welcomed the move, but demand for a dedicated skill training centre remains.

Transport • Daily Update

Erode–Chennai Vande Bharat adds Salem stoppage, reduces travel time by 20 min

Railway authorities have announced an additional stoppage at Salem Junction for train no. 20678 (Erode–Chennai Vande Bharat Express), effective May 1, 2026. The revised schedule will see departure from Erode at 05:50, reaching Salem at 06:45, and Chennai Central at 11:10 – cutting down overall travel time by 20 minutes. The move follows repeated demands from Kongu region passenger associations. Tickets for the new timetable go live from April 18.

Trade & Markets

Turmeric prices surge 10% in Erode market – Nepal demand drives rally

The famous Erode Turmeric Market saw a sharp price increase on Monday, with the modal price touching ₹12,500 per quintal, up 10.2% from last week. Traders attribute the spike to increased export orders from Nepal and Bangladesh ahead of festive seasons. Meanwhile, arrivals dropped by 18% due to off-season conditions. The Erode Turmeric Merchants Association has urged the state to open a dedicated fumigation centre to boost value-added exports.
