Coimbatore News — April 13, 2026
Election 2026

Coimbatore South: Senthil Balaji vs AIADMK vs TVK — most watched seat of 2026

DMK has moved strongman V Senthil Balaji from Karur to Coimbatore South, making it one of the most keenly watched contests of the 2026 Tamil Nadu election. BJP fields Vanathi Srinivasan at Coimbatore North, and TVK contests all 11 seats. With 26.9 lakh voters across 11 constituencies, Coimbatore's outcome could shape the overall balance of power.

📅 April 13, 2026 · 🏷 Elections
Aviation

Coimbatore airport international terminal — committed in DMK manifesto

DMK's 2026 election manifesto specifically commits to building a new international terminal at Coimbatore airport. The business community has long called for better global connectivity to support the city's textile and engineering export sectors. The announcement has been welcomed across industry associations in the Kongu region.

📅 April 12, 2026 · 🏷 Infrastructure
Transport

Coimbatore–Bengaluru Vande Bharat revised schedule — new Ernakulam service also approved

Train 20642 now departs Coimbatore at 07:25, reaching Bengaluru at 13:45. The return service leaves at 14:20. Separately, Railway Ministry has approved a new Vande Bharat connecting Bengaluru and Ernakulam via Coimbatore, Tiruppur, Erode and Salem — giving Kongu unprecedented rail connectivity.
