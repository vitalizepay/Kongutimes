Karur home textiles — ₹12,000 crore exported to 45 countries this fiscal year

Karur's home textile exporters recorded a turnover of ₹12,000 crore in the current fiscal year. The United States, United Kingdom, Germany and Australia remain the top buyers of Karur's bed linen, curtains, towels and kitchen fabrics. The industry employs over 2 lakh workers across the district and surrounding areas, making it one of the most important economic contributors in the Kongu zone.

Karur Vysya Bank posts record ₹1,605 crore profit for 2025-26

Karur Vysya Bank has announced a net profit of ₹1,605 crore for FY 2025-26, a 22% increase year-on-year. A dividend of ₹2.50 per share has been declared. The bank's strong performance reflects the robust trade and business environment in the Karur district and wider Kongu region, which continues to generate significant economic activity.
